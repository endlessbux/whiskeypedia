<p>"Whisky" is an alcoholic drink originating in Scotland and Ireland which can be made from a variety of different grains such as barley or wheat. It is not to be confused with "Whiskey" which originated in the US.</p>

<p>Typically the alcohol content in Whisky is 40% meaning that, if consumed in high quantities, users of the substance begin to act strangely. Side effects include slurred speech and loss of rational judgement.</p>
